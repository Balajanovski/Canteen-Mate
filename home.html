Ordering from the canteen has never been so simple

Order food from your canteen from anywhere as long as you have data.

Why use Canteen Mate

Canteen mate is a small, free app, completely powered off of donations that allows you to purchase items from your canteen, from your phone. Yes, as long as you have data or wifi you can order food from your canteen. Canteen mate comes with data compression to lower your mobile bills, and voice control, for when you're on the run

Who should use Canteen Mate

Anyone who wishes to order food from the ease and comfort of their own phone.

- School students
- University students
- Workers

As long as your canteen is set up to work with Canteen Mate, you can order from your phone.

How was Canteen Mate made

Canteen mate started up as a small idea to make ordering from your canteen a lot easier.

Long queues always bothered us, because, with modern day technology automation is so easy, so, we decided to make Canteen Mate

Canteen mate uses Objective-C and SQL for the iOS app, while it uses Java and SQL for the android app.

The data is processed by a Java applet, located on our server

Code will soon be uploaded to GitHub

How to get canteen mate?

Canteen mate is available on the Google play store and app store for free. On first startup canteen mate will ask you for the name of your canteen, just input it and hey presto, you're ready to order from your canteen.

Well what if my canteen isn't listed?

If your canteen isn't listed it just means that your canteen hasn't set up canteen mate yet. Just ask them to implement the system and you will be able to order food from your canteen from anywhere.

How do I set up canteen mate in my school?

You need to purchase a canteen mate setup kit to be able to utilise canteen mate at your school or workplace canteen. For an extremely cheap price you can buy the canteen mate setup kit, and start canteen mate running in the space of less than an hour after delivery.
